PermissionsExPlus Third-Party Notices
=====================================

components inside its platform runtime jars (merged into the universal
PermissionsExPlus-*.jar artifact). Some libraries are relocated under the
ru.tehkode.libs.* package namespace to avoid classpath conflicts.

This file satisfies attribution requirements for permissively licensed
dependencies bundled with the plugin. See LICENSE for PermissionsExPlus
